Cards, badges & checks

Cards are the workhorse surface — 20px radius, tinted shadow, pointer-tracking tilt on hover. Badges are pill tags; checks are filled circles used for feature lists.

Card variants

Five variants; same shape, different elevation and chrome.

Gray

Default surface with a soft tinted drop shadow. Use for most stand-alone content cards.

pb-card-gray

Hover

Raised composite shadow. Use for interactive product/feature cards.

pb-card-hover

Color

Same elevation as hover, reserved for marketing emphasis.

pb-card-color

Border

Lower elevation, reads as an inset panel inside a section.

pb-card-border

Badge

Tinted fill on color-100 with a color-200 border.

pb-card-badge

Texture

Same base card + texture prop for a subtle top-to-bottom wash.

pb-card-gray + texture
